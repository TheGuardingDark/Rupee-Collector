Rupee Crystal Collector

You will be given a random target number at the start of each game.

Your goal is to reach that number without exceeding it.

In order to do this, you will be given four rupees crystals, each with a random value assigned to it.

Your score will increase each time you click a rupee crystal, but you won't know by how much until you click!
